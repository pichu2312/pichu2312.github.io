Background

Heyo, I'm Jacob, I'm a hobbyist game programmer and designer looking to break into the industry. I graduated from the University of Birmingham in 2023 with first-class honours in Computer Science, since then I've been working on personal game projects whilst being an IT volunteer at a cultural heritage site.

I have a passion for creating games and have been doing it for nearly a decade creating games in Unity, Game Maker Studio, Android Studio, Javascript and more. Using my free time to cultivate the skills I have is an important principle I follow and has led to my continued growth after graduating.
